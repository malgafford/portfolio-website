About

My story

Hello! My name is Mallory Gafford. I’m a designer currently living in beautiful Knoxville, TN.

I graduated with my Bachelors in Business Administration and started working in technical writing. As a technical writer, I learned how to translate technical terms into user-friendly language and focus on the user’s experience which led me to UX/UI design. I started learning more about UX and UI and began CareerFoundry's UI Design Program. I fell in love with UX and UI, and have been working on design projects ever since.I love being able to make technology useful, usable, and beautiful for people and I’m looking for a team who shares that same passion. I love to design and create things, and would love to help build solutions with you!
