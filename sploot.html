Sploot Pet Store

UX/UI Design Case Study - eCommerce Responsive Web App

About the Project

  • Role: Product Designer, UX/UI
  • Duration: August - September 2022
  • Tools: Figma, AdobeXD

Sploot is an ecommerce responsive web app where users can shop for pet products based on their personal needs. Users receive personalized products that they can purchase.

My Process

Objective

  • Brief: Design an ecommerce platform that helps users find products quickly and easily for their specific needs.
  • Solution: Create a responsive web app for pet owners where users can shop personalized pet products for their needs.

The Brand

Sploot- the online pet store where users can find all that their pet needs in a fun and simple way. Sploot makes it easy by curating pet products specific to the user. Sploot is simple, fun, and reliable.

Research

I researched users and competitors to determine the 5 why's for this product.

Ideation

I began the ideation phase by determining 3 key features for this app and sketching out those key functions.

User Flows

After rough sketching, I moved into creating user flow diagrams and low-fidelity wireframes.

Mid-Fidelity Wireframes

To get a better representation of each screen and flow, detail and context was added to the low-fidelity wireframes.

User Testing

I conducted a usability test where users were given a scenario and evaluated on if they were able to complete the task. Users were encouraged to talk through their thinking process and give feedback on the overall feel and functionality of the design.

Scenario: You are a user who wants to search for dog treats and filter the results by customer rating. What steps are you taking to accomplish this task?

Analysis: Once the testing was complete, I took my notes from the testing and analyzed the results I created a table with the users' pain points and my solutions.

Check out the user research and prototype here.

Design

After user testing, I incorporated my user's feedback and my solutions to their pain points in the final design.

Syleguide

I created a styleguide for Sploot in order to keep the design consistent throughout the product.
